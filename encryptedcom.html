Encrypted Communication App

We are the sole distributor of Swiss based REALTYME’s military-grade communication encryption to protects our clients from cyber-espionage while enabling their organization to securely and efficiently collaborate.

RealTyme uses a peer-to-peer approach with an end-to-end encryption architecture that directly connects the communicating parties.
No central servers are involved. Encryption happens on each user device. Only the communicating parties have access to the one-time encryption keys, so only they can decipher the calls and messages. Third-party access, including access by RealTyme staff, is impossible.
